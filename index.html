Biography

  • Scott Ramon Seguro Mescudi was born on January 30, 1984, in Cleveland Ohio, to Elsie Harriet(Banks) and Lindberg Styles Mescudi. At the age of eleven, Cudi lost his father to cancer, which severely impacted his personality and music.

  • Inspired by alternative hip-hop groups, Cudi began rapping towards the end of high school and decided to pursue his musical career, by moving to New York City. After moving in with his uncle in 2004, Cudi found himself working at the BAPE store. While working there, he connected with his future mentor Kanye West. After hearing his early work, West decided to sign him to his label Good Music and this propelled Cudi into mainstream popularity. Cudi's debut album was released September 15, 2009 and was an instant hit. This success followed up with 6 more studio albums and three more collaborative albums, including an "experimental rock album".

  • Kid Cudi's musical style has been labeled introspective and emotional, often experimenting with multi-genre covers, while shifting between rapping and singing. With ventures into acting, fashion, and modeling, Cudi is a natural entrepreneur who is no stranger to hustle and hardwork. Cudi revealed in an interview, January 2013, that he had stopped smoking marijuana after the birth of his child and his frustration with being a figurehead for stoner and drug culture. He revealed that the sudden fame spiraled him into drugs and depression, specifcally anti-depressant medication, which he was prescribed after a failed relationship. In October 2016, Cudi checked himself into rehab for depression and suicidal urges.

  • Cudi has been honored with various awards by helping depressed and suicidal youth through his music. He continues to be a mental health advocate and volunteers with various organizations and charities. His legacy is defined by his music, which has inspired an entire generation of young artists to explore with more emotional themes, never before seen from mainstream rap artists. Cudi was and is a voice for people everywhere who feel alone.
